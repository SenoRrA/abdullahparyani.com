Overhauling the Educational Funnel

Ad-Din / Immense Inc.

Timeline: Q1 2023

Role: Strategic Consultant

Sector: EdTech / Community Learning

The Challenge

The community-based learning platform was experiencing high client churn and unstructured user acquisition pipelines. The creative team lacked an editorial direction, leading to inconsistent messaging.

Research & Strategy

Conducted deep competitive landscape mapping to isolate white space in the market. Formulated a definitive brand voice that bridged educational authority with community trust.

Execution

Cemented operational workflows for the creative team, structured a 90-day digital editorial calendar, and implemented data-driven acquisition campaigns.

Results & Impact

Customer acquisitions reached an all-time high during their fiscal peak. Client churn was reduced to less than one-third of initial Q2 projections.

Lessons Learned: Consistency in editorial voice is mathematically tied to retention.

Scale & Security Operations

Google Operations Centre

Timeline: 2020 - 2021

Role: Customer Operations Analyst

Sector: Big Tech / Trust & Safety

The Challenge

Managing support and security for Google Play, a platform with over 1.5 Billion Monthly Active Users. A localized scam network was bypassing automated Google filters, leading to user financial loss.

Research & Strategy

Manually analyzed flagged transaction clusters to identify the exact behavior patterns the automated systems were missing. Mapped the exploit methodology used by the bad actors.

Execution

Designed and submitted a comprehensive "Scam Prevention Workflow" to the engineering team. Additionally, served on the Social Media Crisis team during the PUBG Mobile Ban crisis.

Results & Impact

The workflow was adopted, shutting down the localized exploit. Handled mass-scale user panic (affecting 76M players) with zero PR escalations.

Lessons Learned: Global scale requires human intuition to catch edge-case anomalies.

Digital Transformation of Brick & Mortar

Khan Al Sultan & OZIS

Timeline: 2023

Role: Digital Architect

Sector: Retail / Hospitality

The Challenge

Traditional businesses relying heavily on foot traffic needed to transition into the digital age to survive local market contractions and reach a broader audience.

Execution

Digitized entire physical inventories. Directed product photography. Built cohesive e-commerce storefronts on modern CMS platforms. Deployed localized SEO and SEM campaigns using Google My Business and targeted ads.

Results & Impact

Bridged decades of traditional goodwill with a modern, high-converting digital funnel, unlocking new revenue streams independent of physical foot traffic.

Lessons Learned: Local intent search is the highest converting traffic for physical retail.
